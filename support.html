Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get paid for completed tasks? +

Payments are processed automatically on the 1st and 15th of each month for all approved tasks. You need a minimum balance of $10 to request a withdrawal. You can choose to receive payments via PayPal, bank transfer, or other available methods in your region.

Why was my bid rejected? +

Bids can be rejected for several reasons: your bid amount may be outside the acceptable range, you may not meet the demographic requirements for the survey, or there may be too many bidders for the available slots. You can always place new bids on other available surveys.

How long does task approval take? +

Task approval typically takes 3-5 business days. Some complex tasks may take up to 7 business days. You'll receive a notification once your task has been reviewed and either approved or rejected with feedback.

Can I withdraw a bid after placing it? +

Yes, you can withdraw a bid as long as it hasn't been accepted yet. Go to "My Bids" page, find the bid you want to withdraw, and click the "Withdraw Bid" button. Once a bid is accepted, you cannot withdraw it and must complete the task.

What happens if I miss a task deadline? +

If you miss a task deadline without requesting an extension, the task may be reassigned to another contributor. This can affect your completion rate and may result in lower priority for future task assignments. If you need more time, contact support before the deadline to request an extension.
