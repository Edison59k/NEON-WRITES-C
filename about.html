About Neon Writers

Empowering Kenyan writers to earn from their skills since 2024.

Our Story

Neon Writers was founded with a simple mission: to create opportunities for Kenyan writers to earn a sustainable income from their writing skills. We recognized the talent pool in Kenya and the growing demand for quality content worldwide.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talented writers and organizations seeking quality content, while ensuring fair compensation, secure payments, and a supportive community for writers to grow their skills and careers.
